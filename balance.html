Your GBP balance comes with a sort code and account number. You'll be able to share these with people who want to send GBP to you. They will avoid hidden fees and bad exchange rates. The Payment Service Act imposes limits on Singapore users of e-wallets on how much money they can send, spend or hold in their account.
