FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I send money with RemitEase? ▼

    To send money with RemitEase, first, create an account on our platform. Once logged in, you can add a recipient by entering their details. Then, initiate a transfer by selecting the recipient, specifying the amount to send, and choosing your preferred payment method. Follow the prompts to complete the transaction securely.

  • How long does a transfer take? ▼

    The time it takes for a transfer to complete depends on several factors, including the destination country and the payment method used. Generally, transfers can be completed within minutes to a few hours. For some countries, it may take up to 1-2 business days. You can track the status of your transfer in your RemitEase account.

  • Does it cost anything to receive money into my RemitEase account? ▼

    Receiving money into your RemitEase account is free of charge. However, please be aware that there may be fees associated with transferring money from your RemitEase account to your bank account or for certain types of transfers. You can view the specific fees applicable to each transaction in your account settings.

  • Can I have more than 1 account? ▼

    No, each individual is allowed to maintain only one RemitEase account for security and compliance reasons. If you need to update your account details or have any issues, please contact our customer support team for assistance.

  • How do I activate my account after registration? ▼

    After registering on RemitEase, you will receive an One Time Password (OTP). To activate your account, enter the OTP provided in the email. If you do not receive the email within a few minutes, please check your spam folder or contact our support team for help.
