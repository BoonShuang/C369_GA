PERSONAL DATA PROTECTION ACT

The PDPA refers to the Personal Data Protection Act. It is a data protection regulation mandated by the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS). The PDPA sets out rules and regulations for the collection, use, disclosure, and protection of personal data in Singapore. It aims to safeguard individuals' personal data and ensure that organizations handle personal data responsibly and securely. Compliance with the PDPA is crucial for organizations operating in Singapore to protect the privacy and rights of individuals.

RemitEase prioritizes the protection of our users' personal and financial information.
